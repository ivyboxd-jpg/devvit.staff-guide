The
Role Execution Guide.

Operational instructions for all Mentors and Volunteers. Follow this guide to ensure a smooth mission flow.

Designation: Liaison

Volunteer

As a Volunteer, you are the backbone of the event operations. You are expected to be available for the full duration of the 3-day hackathon.

DAY 01 — PREP

Webinar & Briefing

  • Attend the staff webinar to understand the logistics.
  • Get to know your assigned team and their background.
  • Read the Prep Kit and all problem statements thoroughly.
DAY 02 — THE BUILD

Operational Ground Control

  • Ensure teams submit their project proposals on time.
  • Check in on team morale and progress every few hours.
  • Critical: Escalate any major blockers to Raghav or Shradha immediately.
DAY 03 — CLOSING

Final Delivery

  • Help teams with last-minute submission technicalities.
  • Ensure final links and assets are uploaded before the 7 PM deadline.

✓ YOUR FOCUS

  • Monitor team health and timeline adherence.
  • Flag issues to leadership early.
  • Keep teams excited and focused.

✗ NOT YOUR FOCUS

  • Writing code or fixing technical bugs.
  • Judging the final projects.
  • Making major rule changes on your own.
Designation: Expert

Mentor

Your expertise is needed during the most critical phases of the build. You will be on-call to support teams during Round 1 (Ideation) and Round 2 (Core Build).

ROUND 01 — PROPOSAL

Strategic Feedback

  • Help teams validate their project idea against the problem statement.
  • Help teams define their MVP (Minimum Viable Product).
  • Advise on the best tech stack for their specific logic.
ROUND 02 — CORE BUILD

Technical Support

  • Be reachable on WhatsApp for specific technical questions.
  • Help unblock teams stuck on Devvit API or general logic bugs.
  • Guide teams to simplify their scope if they are running behind.

✓ YOUR FOCUS

  • Asking the "hard" logic questions.
  • Providing technical direction.
  • Simplifying complex project ideas.

✗ NOT YOUR FOCUS

  • Doing the actual coding for the team.
  • Managing team schedules or logistics.
  • Tracking submission deadlines.
