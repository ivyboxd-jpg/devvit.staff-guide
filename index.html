Volunteer Liaison

Operational anchor. You track deadlines and escalate issues to Command.

Timeline & Duties
Build Phase Operations ▼
  • Confirm team proposal submission by 9:00 AM Saturday.
  • Conduct active check-ins every 3 hours.
  • Monitor for "Scope Creep" and keep the build focused.
  • Final submission verification by 7:00 PM Sunday.
✓ Liaison Dos
  • Flag team blockers immediately to Raghav or Shradha.
  • Keep the team's morale high and focus sharp.
  • Ensure all team members are active and present.
✗ Liaison Don'ts
  • Do not perform technical work or write code.
  • Do not try to solve major conflicts alone.
  • Do not miss a scheduled check-in window.
Expert Mentor

Technical Consultant. You provide logic guidance and architectural support.

Mentorship Scope
Saturday Support Flow ▼
  • Be reachable for team consultations during build hours.
  • Perform a proactive review of the team's technical logic.
  • Unblock Devvit API or platform-specific hurdles.
✓ Mentor Dos
  • Ask questions that challenge the team's assumptions.
  • Guide teams toward an achievable MVP.
  • Provide directional advice on API integration.
  • Notify Command if a team's tech stack is failing.
✗ Mentor Don'ts
  • Do not write the code for the participants.
  • Do not make product decisions on their behalf.
  • Do not evaluate or score the final projects.
  • Do not do the thinking for them—guide it.
